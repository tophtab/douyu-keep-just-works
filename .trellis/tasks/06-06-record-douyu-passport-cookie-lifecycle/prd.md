# Record Douyu passport cookie lifecycle

## Goal

Record the verified Douyu Passport, main-site, and Yuba cookie lifecycle contracts discovered during live diagnostics so future auth/cookie changes do not reintroduce CookieCloud authority conflicts or incorrect assumptions about long-lived credentials.

## What I already know

- The user wants this knowledge captured in Trellis, not a runtime fix in this task.
- Live diagnostics showed QR login issues a long-lived `passport.douyu.com` `LTP0` cookie and shorter-lived main/Yuba business cookies.
- Live diagnostics showed the main-site business cookie is effectively single-active for a given passport/device: after signing a second main cookie from the same `LTP0`, the first main cookie failed fans/backpack validation with `error: 9` / `请登录`.
- Live diagnostics showed Yuba cookies are reissued during Yuba SSO and values change; the old Yuba cookie was not immediately invalidated in the observed validation, but must not be treated as durable because many Yuba calls also depend on a current main-site `dy-token`.
- CookieCloud/browser cookies and backend Passport refresh must not both act as independent write authorities for local main-site cookies.

## Requirements

- Add a backend code-spec entry documenting:
  - Passport `LTP0` as the upstream Web SSO credential.
  - Main-site `acf_*` / `dy_auth` as shorter-lived business cookies minted from Passport.
  - Yuba `acf_yb_*` as shorter-lived business cookies minted through Passport + main-site SSO.
  - Main-site business cookies are effectively single-active under the observed flow.
  - Passport is long-lived but not permanent, and only successful safeAuth/SSO proves it is still service-valid.
  - Recovery should refresh main and Yuba together from Passport rather than preserving stale Yuba when full recovery is required.
- Keep secrets out of specs and logs.
- Do not change runtime code in this task.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `.trellis/spec/backend` contains a documented auth/cookie lifecycle contract.
- [ ] `.trellis/spec/backend/index.md` links to the new or updated spec.
- [ ] The spec includes concrete contracts, validation behavior, good/bad cases, and test requirements.
- [ ] No runtime source files are changed.

## Out of Scope

- Implementing automatic Passport refresh or Yuba recovery changes.
- Adding tests for those runtime changes.
- Changing CookieCloud behavior.
